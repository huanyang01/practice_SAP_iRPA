Want to make a positive impact by making just one small change?

Take something as simple as the search engine you use. Every search you do on the internet generates advertising revenue which collectively adds up to around €98 million a year. But instead of banking that revenue, how about a search engine that uses that money for the power of good?

At Lilo, we let you use that money to fund social and environmental projects. You get to decide where the money you generate with advertising goes. Whether that’s preventing deforestation in the Amazon, supporting street kids or protecting the turtles from poaching, you get to support projects and causes you care about simply through your searches. Lilo is the search engine designed to provide great search results and do some good along the way. So why not switch your search engine and join our mission today?

That's a very good question. It's exactly the question we asked ourselves when we were setting up Lilo.

A little known fact is that search engines generate an awful lot of money every year through the sponsored links and ads at the top of every page of search results. Globally, this figure is around 98 billion Euros. 98 billion Euros. Just from people searching for the best ice cream recipe or where the nearest post office is. And who generates that money? You do.

At Lilo, we wanted to create a search engine that allows you to control the money you generate and spend it on things that matter most to you. So we decided to develop a search engine that would allow users to take control of the money generated by their web searches and use it to fund whichever social and environmental projects they fancied. redistributes half its advertising income to life changing social and environmental projects across the world whilst respecting our users privacy along the way. So that's what we've done. 50% of all money generated goes towards the different social and environmental projects we support. The other 50% helps to maintain the proper functioning of our search engine so that you always have the fastest, most relevant results.

Since we launched, Lilo has already helped to allocate 2.8 million Euros to the projects we fund. And this is just the beginning...

Absolutely. Our mission is to help you quickly find everything you're looking for on the internet. We don't compromise on results relevance and use the same algorithms as the largest search engines. Lilo is different though so it may take a couple of days to get used to. For instance, your previous search engine may have stored your favourite websites by tracking your clicks for certain searches. Because we don't have access to that information, we'll need to get to know you better. Don't worry, it'll happen quickly, but you can expect a tiny algorithm experience curve. There may also be some small differences on some specific features. For example, if you search for the weather forecast on Lilo, you will be redirected to a website showing the weather forecast, while other search engines can show it directly. This direct answer is not available yet on Lilo but it's something we're working on. Same goes for voice control.

Searching with Lilo means you can find what you want and do some good along the way.

. Privacy policies on the web can be as hard to trust as the gossip columns. That's why we are committed to being transparent about ours. We've all had that unsettling feeling of being tracked on the internet. With Lilo, this is impossible. We don't accept any scripts or third party cookies. Nor do we sell your search data to any advertising agencies or anyone else for that matter.

  1. With Lilo, your searches are never sold or spied on
  2. Your privacy is your business and your personal data is just that - personal.
  3. You choose what tracking and statistics you're comfortable with

That means you can choose to support the fight against Amazon deforestation, help disadvantaged children in your local area, protect turtles in Mayotte from poaching and more.
